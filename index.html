About Brandon

Welcome to my site! Here you will learn about my skills, iterests, and goals.

I love telling stories through digital design. Posters, logos, shirts, advertisements, or anything creative. This isn't only a passion for me, but also something I would love to do as a career.

Creating podcasts allows me to share my ideas, and also pushes me to continue learning new content. I have been active on a sports podcast, and now podcast once a week regarding technology and design.

When not working I enjoy playing games. I am fond of new games, but also love playing classic retro games.

Exercise and fresh air are important. When not working on a computer, I like to get away from the screen and golf. Golfing with my daughter is my favorite thing to do.
